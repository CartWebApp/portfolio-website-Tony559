Development Log

A log of my "daily" activities for programming

3/21/25 - - Worked on story elements, making the story diagram in LucidChart, here: Link. This was done with the help of Schroeder, my partner. We have discussed a starting point for the story, making hatsune miku, a vocaloid, the villain of the story. We have decided that we want her to come out of a portal, fighting master oogway, and intends to make this world hers. This is a reference to her song/song lyric “world is mine”.
3/24/25 - - Worked on the flow chart, did a lot more than the 21st. First, we planned two secret endings and made sure to find lines that would work with them. Second, we found reference images to figure out how exactly we were going to represent our ideas in the game. Next, we split up, trying to develop the main endings so that we could split off into the secret endings in the first place. We also devised our gameplay elements, deciding that we would have an inventory, relationship system, and rhythm game battles. I also looked at examples of how people coded their rhythm games in JavaScript. Here is the source I used, (I have not created an account yet) Source.
3/25/25 - - Today, Schroeder and I planned what we were going to do. We were figuring out how we wanted to implement certain paths. Next, we each worked on the designated path that we agreed to work on, and after, we reviewed with each other. We did have to make some changes or adjustments to certain paths, but after that, it was smooth sailing. The power went out as we were working on the end of some of the paths so we did not get everything done that we wanted to. We also discussed LeBron James having the ability to transcend time, this also means in any timeline, LeBron James
3/26/25 - - Today, we made sure to re discuss main mechanics in the game to make sure that they are correct. We also made some new secret endings. Another thing that we did was to make sure that we were on the right track to fulfill the requirements for the first milestone.
3/27/25 - - Schroeder was not here today, so I worked on an ending for the storyline, and found a lot of inspiration for my personal portfolio website. I found a website that is basically a windows desktop view, but online. I also found a website that has a lot of interactivity on the mouse, the background has particles that follow the mouse.
3/28/25 - - Today, Schroeder and I both completed all of our endings in the storyline, we made sure to agree on how functions work. We also worked on our personal portfolio research, and finished up our program flowchart.
3/31/25 - - We both took half the time today to work on our portfolio. In the second half of the period today, we worked on our game. I worked on the story google docs document and Schroeder worked on the code development. After I got burnt out on the story, I tried to find an AI that would allow me to make a Hatsune Miku voice with text to speech, but I could not find a free one that was not blocked by CART.

One thing that I did completely wrong, is that I started with my hi-fi figma before my lo-fi figma. I made a lot of progress with the hi-fi though, and found my theme and how I want it to look.

4/3/25 - - Me and the schroeder got a lot done today. I helped him debug his framework for the code and got a lot of the story document done. One thing that I regretted was putting so much emphasis on chatgpt. Instead, I will be putting the rest of my time toward the story document and just copy paste what we had on the flowchart.
4/4/25 - - Today was just about the most productive day this week, we completed the story document, linking each part together flawlessly. There was not much that we could have done better today, except for maybe making sure that we both had the same formatting for the document. There is a slight issue about the formatting but the total framework and functionality is the same so it works correctly.
4/7/25 - - Today, I began working in the rhythm game portion of the game, and it went swimmingly, I worked on its framework and I believe that it is slightly over halfway done. I regret not working on my portfolio today, as the deadline is approaching very soon.
4/8/25 - - I just about finished the framework for the rhythm game, and have notes falling down. I found a flappy bird-ish game and reworked it's horizontal scrolling engine to vertical.
4/9/25 - - An extremely productive day, I made sure that the engine was consistent across devices by mapping things to the audio instead of off frames, because some computers generate frames faster than others. I also made sure that the speed of the notes falling down was independent from the rest of the game.
4/10/25 - - Today was mostly spent on the portfolio. I realized that the figma that I had made was not based off the mood board, so I had to redo the figma. I also realized that the first one was not made to have a lot of different pages, which is what the portfolio needs.

4/23/25 - - Today was mostly spent making the user interface for the story and the rhythm game. Before this, it was plain and simple, in order to make coding the game itself easier. Now, however, it is clean and looks easy for users to navigate.
4/24/25 - (minecraft day) - Worked on the transition from the story to the rhythm game. After working on this, I worked on a way for the rhythm game to end. Next, we went to go see the Minecraft movie, taking up most of the day.
4/25/25 - - Today was mostly spent finding a way for the scripts to talk to each other. This is due to the fact that the scripts of the rhythm game and the story are independent from each other. I regret not getting more sleep the day before.
4/28/25 - - Today we worked on ui elements and made sure the story could connect through the rhythm game. We also started working on the final draft of the presentation.

I have completely fixed everything about the rhythm game, touch screen support is completed, and I have added media queries that should work for everything. The text may need to get smaller but I feel that it is fine how it is at the moment. The time is currently 10:57, see you on the other side o7.
