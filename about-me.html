Introduction

Because of my strong foundation in piano and music, I started to develop a deep appreciation for rhythm games and coding, since they both exhibited skills from the piano, such as timing, patterns, and precision. This hobby for rhythm games became a passion, and I began to program my own game with rhythmic aspects and creativity. I consider this my most impressive accomplishment in terms of coding simply due to the fact that it combines all my skills of problem-solving, focus, timing, patterns, and brews it into a beautiful project.
